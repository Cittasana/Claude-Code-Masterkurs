- **Beispiele**: Konkrete Use-Cases (Vertragsanalyse, Spec-Reviews, Research-Synthesis) statt Toy-Examples.

- **Authority-Signal**: Screenshots der Desktop-UI, konkrete Project-Settings, Custom-Instruction-Snippets.
